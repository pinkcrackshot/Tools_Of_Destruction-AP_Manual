# Tools Of Destruction - AP Manual
An Archipelago Manual for Ratchet &amp; Clank (Future): Tools of Destruction.

Very early in development. Not tested past Fastoon - some basic checks are in place, but softlocks beyond this point are likely. Softlocks before Fastoon are less likely, but may still be possible. Report issues to the Discord thread for ToD on the Manual Discord.

Information needed to play:
- This Manual is designed to start from a fresh save, no challenge mode.
- All base movement tech (double/long/high jump, grav/grind boots) as well as wrench usage is allowed from the get-go, as all of it is required far too early to warrant restricting. The charge boots, however, are collected later on and are not key to progression*, so their use is restricted until the necessary Permit is obtained.
- Items requiring Permit may be bought/picked up at any time, but may not be used until the Permit is obtained. Items requiring Unlock may not be purchased until the Unlock is received (this mainly applies to Armor, which will be automatically equipped on purchase and so must not be purchased early.)
- Progressive Travel items allow you to willingly choose to travel to the next area. This means leaving with the Smuggler on Cobalia requires a Progressive Travel item, however escaping in the Imperial Cruiser or Escape Pod does not. With Aphelion: Travelling to a new planet counts, transitioning out of a space level does not, eg. selecting Mucow from Fastoon requires a Progressive Travel, but travelling through the Voron Asteroid Belt to get there does not incur a second Progressive Travel requirement. Returning to any planet later also does not incur a Progressive Travel requirement: mandatory returns to Mucow and Fastoon are free, just as any other optional backtrack is.

The following edits to logic have been made for ease of play:
- While the game can be beaten with only the Wrench, you will always receive a Permit for an accessible Weapon or Device that will attack or disarm shielded enemies before Kortog (essentially, anything available to that point besides the Combuster), so you are not FORCED to play a majority of the game with only the Wrench. You may still choose to do so if you wish, you madman. (A setting may be added later that determines what, if anything, will be received eg. receiving the Fusion Grenade Permit or the Groovitron Permit here both work, but one is considerably easier to make continued use of than the other. At present this is outside of my limited capabilities.)
- While most gadgets require a Permit to use, the Heli-Pods have been excluded from this requirement as it is possible to softlock oneself in the Heli-Pod tutorial room if the permit is not obtained before entering the arena. If other gadgets are found to do this in the future, I will also exclude them. 

The following is NOT accounted for and may cause softlocks:
- Checks missed before leaving Cobalia MAY cause softlocks before repairing Aphelion on Fastoon (eg. if the Robo-Wings Permit is locked behind one of the optional Leviathan Souls.) This may be adjusted in the future, but for now, simply ensure all checks are completed on Cobalia before leaving.
- As mentioned above, this Manual remains completely untested beyond Fastoon, and there WILL be softlocks until I am able to work through the entire game and polish the logic.

Goal: Defeat Tachyon.

Items:
- Permits for Weapons, Devices and Gadgets (allows usage, not tied to ownership)
- Progressive Armor Unlocks (buy the armor after unlocking)
- Progressive Travel (does not affect forced transitions eg. Kerwan to Cobalia, Voron to Mukow etc.)
- Filler: Bolts and Raritanium if you want to save-edit it in.

Checks:
- Obtaining Weapons, Devices and Gadgets (finding/purchasing, does not grant usability)
- Fully upgrading weapons with Raritaium (not tied to usability)
- Reaching V5 with weapons (IS tied to usability)
- Purchasing Armor (once unlocked)
- Gold Bolts, Holo-Plans, Leviathan Souls, Lombax Flight Components (more assorted collectibles TBA)
- Skill Points (mistakenly labelled Challenges for now)
- Bolt savings milestones

Planned Features:
- Adjustable goal
- Optional Arena challenge checks
- Settings to enable/disable Weapon Upgrade requirements as they can be grind-y, especially the V5 checks
- Settings to lower/raise earlygame difficulty by guaranteeing/pushing back early Weapon Permits
- Better softlock protection, both throughout the relatively-untouched lategame, and throughout earlygame (aforementioned concerns regarding missed cobalia checks)
